## Directory Description

Workflow Triage Organism is a small AI-assisted diagnostic for repetitive business workflows. It helps operators, founders, agencies, and freelancers decide which process is worth automating first. The local diagnostic scores repetition, time cost, rule clarity, and error risk. Paid triage can turn a workflow into a map, opportunity ranking, risk notes, prompt pack, SOP, or lightweight prototype.
